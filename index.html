About Us

Sree Narayana Gurukulam College of Engineering, established in 2002, is located in Kadayirippu, Ernakulam. Affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University, the college is dedicated to delivering high-quality engineering education with a strong foundation in values and social commitment.
